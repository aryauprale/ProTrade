Investment accounts to choose from

Different investment account types serve different goals like retirement, education, and more.

  • Stocks: Invest in companies and potentially earn dividends/capital gains.
  • Bonds: Lend to governments or corporations for interest payments.
  • Mutual Funds: Diversified pooled investments managed as a portfolio.
  • ETFs: Like mutual funds but traded on exchanges.
  • REITs: Real estate exposure without direct ownership.
  • Cryptocurrencies: Digital assets like Bitcoin and Ethereum (higher risk).
