Account types

Investment accounts to choose from

Different investment account types support different financial goals like retirement, education, diversification, and long-term wealth building.

  • Stocks: Invest in companies and potentially earn capital gains or dividends.
  • Bonds: Lend to governments or corporations in exchange for interest payments.
  • Mutual Funds: Access diversified pooled investments managed as a portfolio.
  • ETFs: Trade diversified funds on exchanges with flexibility and convenience.
  • REITs: Gain real estate exposure without directly owning property.
  • Cryptocurrencies: Explore digital assets like Bitcoin and Ethereum as higher-risk options.
